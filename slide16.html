Signs I Failed

I've had multiple conversations where:
  • A more junior engineer only knows a single framework.
  • A peer was not aware of the built in functionality of the web. I didn't teach them.
  • I pushed customers into overly complex solutions because it was my prefered way to work.
  • I encouraged solving the problem at hand rather than creating understanding about the web in general. Deadlines were the most important thing.

We have a moral responsibility to keep the web accessible and to keep the web simple. We need to teach the next generation of the platform. We need to teach them why the abstractions exist and how we got there. We need to help them fail faster than we have so they don't spend years making the same mistakes we have.
