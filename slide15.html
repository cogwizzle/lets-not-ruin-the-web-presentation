Who is to Blame?

Me. Why?
  • I got too excited about the promise of reusable components.
  • I chased ever shiny framework under the sun.
  • It took me 10 years of framework chasing to ask is this getting easier? It wasn't. Building websites and web apps was harder than ever.
  • Single Page Apps provided a superior navigation experience. At the expense of replicating browser navigation and breaking native browser behavior.
  • I became enamored with JavaScript's evolution and being good at JavaScript became a badge of honor. Its still my favorite language.
