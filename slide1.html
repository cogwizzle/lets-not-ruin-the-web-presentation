Today

The web has evolved to be way larger than what its intended use case. Engineers are driving towards complexity at extreme cost.

Symptoms:
  • Complicated
  • Inaccessible
  • Not Resilient
  • Slow

The point of this talk is not to talk down at my peers about their technology choices. The point is to bring awareness to some real problems we've created in the last 10 years. I want to talk about our responsibility as creators to guide the web to a better place.
