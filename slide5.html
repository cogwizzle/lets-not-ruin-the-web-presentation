Platform Equivalent without JavaScript

Here is the built in platform equivalent without JavaScript. It uses the browsers built in loading spinner. It doesn't require any state management outside of what the browser provides in the form. 


<div class="flash">Unable to authenticate.</div>
<form method="post" action="/auth/login">
    <input type="hidden" name="csrf-token" id="csrf-token" value="random_token" />
    <label for="username">Username</label>
    <input type="text" name="username" id="username" />
    <label for="password">Password</label>
    <input type="password" name="password" id="password" />
    <button type="submit">Login</button>
</form>
